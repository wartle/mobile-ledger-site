Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Mobile Ledger is built with your privacy in mind. We believe your financial data should remain yours.

  • Data Collection: We do not collect or store your financial transactions on our servers. All data is stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud storage.
  • Third Parties: We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
  • Security: We recommend using FaceID/TouchID features within the app to secure your local data.
