Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

Mobile Ledger ("we," "our," or "the app") is a personal finance management application designed to help you track expenses, income, and manage your financial accounts. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how your data is handled.

Summary

  • Your data stays on your device - All financial data is stored locally using Apple's SwiftData framework
  • No cloud sync by default - CloudKit synchronization is disabled
  • No third-party tracking - We do not use analytics services or share your data
  • Optional backup - You can manually backup/restore your data via iCloud
  • Biometric protection - Optional Face ID/Touch ID authentication for app access

Data We Collect

Financial Data (Stored Locally)

The app stores the following data exclusively on your device:

  • Transactions: Date, amount, description, type (expense/income/transfer), and category
  • Accounts: Account names, types, balances, associated bank/institution
  • Categories: Custom and default spending/income categories
  • Budgets: Budget amounts and thresholds
  • Ledgers: Organizational groupings for your financial data
  • Currencies: Currency preferences and exchange information
  • Custom Icons: Any custom icons you upload for accounts

User Preferences

We store your app preferences locally using Apple's standard storage:

  • Display preferences (currency format, theme)
  • Privacy settings (auto-lock timeout, biometric authentication)
  • Notification preferences
  • Account visibility settings

Biometric Data

If you enable Face ID or Touch ID authentication:

  • We do not store biometric data - Authentication is handled entirely by Apple's secure enclave
  • We only store a preference flag indicating whether biometric lock is enabled

How Your Data Is Stored

Local Storage

All your financial data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device automatically
  • Data is protected by your device's built-in encryption
  • Data is only accessible through the Mobile Ledger app

iCloud Backup (Optional)

The app provides an optional manual backup feature:

  • You can create backups stored in your personal iCloud account
  • Backups are encrypted and protected by your Apple ID credentials
  • You control when backups are created and restored
  • We cannot access your iCloud backup data

Note: Standard iCloud device backups may include app data. You can manage this in your device's iCloud settings.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect or transmit personal identification information
  • We do not use third-party analytics or tracking services
  • We do not sell, share, or rent your data to third parties
  • We do not access your bank accounts or financial institutions
  • We do not store data on external servers
  • We do not use advertising networks

Data Security

We implement the following security measures:

  • Local-only storage: Data remains on your device
  • Biometric authentication: Optional Face ID/Touch ID protection
  • Auto-lock: Configurable automatic lock when the app enters the background
  • Balance hiding: Option to hide sensitive balance information in the UI
  • Apple's security framework: Leveraging iOS built-in encryption and secure enclave

Your Rights and Controls

You have complete control over your data:

Access and Export

  • View all your data within the app
  • Export transactions to a ZIP archive containing JSON files for personal use

Deletion

  • Delete individual transactions, accounts, or categories at any time
  • Delete the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Manage iCloud backups through your Apple ID settings

Privacy Controls

  • Enable/disable biometric authentication
  • Configure auto-lock timeout
  • Hide account balances from view
  • Exclude accounts from net worth calculations

Children's Privacy

Mobile Ledger does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for personal finance management by adults.

Third-Party Services

Apple Services

The app uses the following Apple services:

  • SwiftData: Local data storage (no data transmitted)
  • iCloud: Optional backup storage (encrypted, controlled by your Apple ID)
  • Face ID/Touch ID: Biometric authentication (processed in secure enclave)
  • Local Notifications: Reminder notifications (processed on-device)

No third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms are used.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Providing notice within the app for significant changes

Data Retention

  • Local data is retained until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • iCloud backups follow your Apple ID's iCloud storage policies
  • We do not retain any data on external servers

Region-Specific Provisions

For Users in the European Economic Area (EEA)

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, and object to processing, as well as the right to data portability. Since all data is stored locally, you exercise these rights directly through the app's features.

For Users in California

Under CCPA, California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, delete it, and opt-out of its sale. We do not sell personal information.

This privacy policy applies to Mobile Ledger for iOS.
