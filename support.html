Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?
All your financial data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. It never leaves your device unless you choose to create a manual backup.

How do I backup my data?
You can create manual backups to your iCloud account through the settings menu in the app. This ensures you can restore your data if you switch devices.

Is my data secure?
Yes. We leverage iOS built-in encryption and offer optional biometric (Face ID/Touch ID) protection to keep your financial information private.
